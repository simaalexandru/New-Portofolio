Online Campaign

For our final project in the second semester, we have been asked to create a marketing campaign and a landing page for Lulu’s Drawer. Lulu’s Drawer is a new fashion brand, scheduled to launch in July 2016. Our marketing campaign will focus on the following end results: brand awareness and sales. The purpose of this assignment is to demonstrate that we have developed the academic skills expected in the curriculum for the first year of Multimedia Design and Communication course.

Our final idea was to create awareness for our client using social media and by creating a landing page that would both create visibility and generate sales.The important part of the landing page is the Call to Action, wich is the newsletter subscription form.We want the user to subscribe to our newsletter and also to be able to find information about the company and photos of the products on the same page.

We also created a Facebook page and an Instagram account.Lulu's Drawer Facebook page will be used to inform the followers on upcoming events,new products giveaways, sales.Since we have a goal of 5000 likes on Facebook, we will use the "Page Like" ad on Facebook.A static banner ad was developed for advertisments via online magazines.Also, by subscribing on the landing page, potential customers will automatically be subscribed to the Lulu's Drawer Landing newsletter.The initial letter will include a personal welcome, information on the company and information on the introduction campaign.We also created a flyer that will be used in the retail outlets that sell the product, plus it could be included in the packages that are sent to the customers who have purchased products.

All on all this assignment took us far and wide within the field of Multimedia Design and Communication and thereby we have been given the possibility to display the academic skills we have acquired during these 2 first semesters.

Service Design

The State Museum of Art Copenhagen (SMK) has invited Cphbusiness students to suggest and re-new their portofolio of customer journeys.In mixed teams we had to identify a given challenge and analyse, research and design solutions to be presented in the Ignite format.

Our initial idea is basically to incoporate a museum guide into your phone via an app. The app itself consist of several premade touring guides, that you can pick between where the color represent different art styles, the route is created so you will always finish where you started, even with the option to combine several routes together to create a unique personal tour. If you're dissatisfied with the current routes, you got the option to create your own completely personal route, where you can choose where to start, what to skip and when to end.

Finally a snapchat function will be implemented which will allow the visitors to take pictures without having to worry about flash issues, as well as editing the pictures they take to create their own art work, and sharing it with their friends for a few laughs to encourage more youths to visit.

We decided to go with an app because it appeals to the children & young adults who wants more and more technology with every passing moment, and gives them a way to try to learn their parents, rather than learning from them which is the usual way the information sharing goes on family trips

Database Website

The objective of this project was to work with dynamic web pages and basic content management: We had to develop a small scale blog for a design related topic.This project was focusing on the technical and design aspects of dynamic web pages.The frontend of the blog should be a single page displaying the publishing articles in well-defined section.The visual design should reflect the chosen subject.

During this project I learned the "backend" programming with PHP, basic principles of MySQL databases, dinamic rendering of database records in the HTML frontend and accessing databases using standard (PHPmyAdmin) and custom (PHP/HTML) client interfaces.

Our final idea was to create a blog about logo-design.After we chose the topic we started to write the articles and create a template wich later on will contain the articles.We created blog with mixed content (static and dynamic HTML) with three articles about logo-design.Each post has a headline, related image, the date of posting and the author's name.This blog has responsive design, appropriate user feedback and JavaScript form check.In this project we focused more on the technical part.

Online Magazine

The goal of this project was to create an e-zine on a subject of our own choice.We had to design the visual identity, layout and content of the magazine.We had to research and justify the time and costs needed to produce the magazine for the target audience and also the application icon that would be used on an iPhone or Android.

During this project I learned what is an online magazine, how to create one and how to find the subject of the magazine, based on the target audience.I learned how to use Adobe InDesign and during the project period I created a 18 page E-magazine.I also used Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator but mainly I learned how to create an Interactive PDF from scratch.I watched a lot of Lynda tutorials and I learned a lot of shortcuts and how to use new tools.I learned how grids work and how you create an issue from a sketch to an online publication.

Our final idea was to create a modern, inspiring and exclusive online travel and lifestyle magazine, called TRVL MGZ, that would be aimed at an audience that has a sense of quality and would prefer an authentic experience when traveling.Since we decided that each one of us should create a separate issue we all got a lot of experience from this project.We decided to create different editions of TRVL MGZ.I decided to create an issue about Romania, my homecountry.

CSR Developement

We had to develop a prototype for a website where a company in the best possible way communicates its social commitment.The company has a need for a redesign of its website developed specifically for the current communication needs.The Corporate Social Responsability is a form of corporate self-regulation integred into a business model.The goal of the CSR is to embrace responsability for the company's actions and encourage a positive impact through its activities on the environment, consumers, employees,communities, stakeholders and all other members of the public sphere.

Our final decision was to create a website for Nemlig.com and focus on their partnership with FødevareBanken, because they do not promote this partnership on their existing website. Additionally, we have added the option that their customers can “round up” to the nearest round number, allowing the extra money to be donated to FødevareBanken.During this project I learned what is Corporate Social Responsability and why it is so important for a company.

The most important thing that I learned during this project it is what is behind a web-browser.How web-pages are ranked and why Google prefer a page instead of another.I learned what is Search Engine Optimization and how do you actually make a web-page more "friendly" for browsers.

Fanta Campaign

In this project we had to develop a concept in collaboration with "Another",a danish company.Our main priority was to come up with great ideas and then find a way to communicate them.We had to transpose our ideas in something visual.

We came up with a lot of ideas but since the target group ages 13-20 we agreed that Fanta’s next campaign includes: television advertisements, bus shelters, bill boards,posters,online banner ads and the brand presents six limited edition cans using QR-codes and Augmented Reality to involve the consumers and invite them to have fun and be creative. We reasearched and we focused on Germany,France and Italy, since the target group was European and Coca-Cola changed the logo in 2009 and we decided not to this and inspired by the Lego 3D catalogue we agreed to go with on the cans, packaging and banners.

It was hard to decide wich idea is better and why to use this one instead of that one but we managed to agree on something. The Augumented Reality campaign will focus on creativity through various forms of media advertising for Europe. New artwork has been designed for the cans, as well as new packaging to create a colourful six-pack.
